How to Play

  • Feed hungry pandas by clicking on them when they show the hungry icon
  • Play with bored pandas by clicking on them when they show the play icon

  • Keep pandas happy or they'll get sad, then angry, then leave!
  • Game ends when 3 pandas leave
  • Score 1 point for each feed and play action
