Once upon a time, a man pooped so much that he pooped out a poop planet full of poop monsters, and he needed to clean up his mess.

Game Rules

Controls:

  • A / ← Arrow Key: Move left
  • D / → Arrow Key: Move right
  • W / ↑ Arrow Key: Jump
  • S / ↓ Arrow Key: Duck
  • Spacebar or Mouse: Shoot laser

Objective:

  • Defeat all poop monsters and bosses
  • Don't let monsters touch you or you'll lose a life
  • You have 3 lives total
  • Each boss requires multiple hits to defeat
